Problems: If you ride a bike up a hill at 10 mph and down the hill at 20 mph, what it your average speed?

$d=10t_{1}$

$d=20t_{2}$
so,
	$t_{1}=\frac{d}{10}$ and $t_2=\frac{d}{20}$

$\frac{d}{t}=r$

 $\frac{2d}{\frac{d}{10}+\frac{d}{20}}$
 
this is equal to,

$\frac{2d}{\frac{3d}{20}}$

which equates to,

$$\frac{40}{3} mph$$
